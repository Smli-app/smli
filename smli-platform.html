How It Works

A real financial life.
In the classroom.

Students don't just learn about money — they experience it through a simulation that mirrors real life.

1
Choose Your Profession
Doctor, Farmer, Software Engineer, Street Food Vendor — each with different income and risk levels.
2
Earn Income Through Activities
Teacher runs activities. Score determines monthly income. Better performance = higher earnings.
3
Pay Real Expenses Every Month
Rent, food, transport, utilities — all proportional to income. No one escapes expenses.
4
Build Emergency Fund First
Only students who save 3 months of expenses unlock investments. The correct financial order.
5
Face Life Events
Medical emergency, vehicle breakdown, storm damage — same event hits everyone differently based on preparation.
What Students Learn

Lessons adults
wish they had

💰
Income & Tax
How salary is earned, why tax is deducted, and what take-home pay really means.
🏠
Fixed Expenses
Rent, food, transport hit every month. No choice. Proportional to income.
🟢
Emergency Fund
3 months of expenses saved before anything else. The foundation of financial safety.
📈
Investments
FD, Mutual Fund, SIP — unlocked only after emergency fund is built.
🛡️
Insurance
One medical event can wipe out years of savings. Insurance prevents that.
💳
Loans
Good debt vs bad debt. EMI pressure. Why banks reject lifestyle loans.
